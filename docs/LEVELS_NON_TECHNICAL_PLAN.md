# Baakhapaa — Levels System Non-Technical Plan

> **Inspired by:** Betterfly's habit-to-impact gamification model  
> **Reference:** [further.group/work/betterfly](https://www.further.group/work/betterfly)

---

## The Big Idea

Betterfly built a platform where **small daily habits snowball into big, visible impact.** Every positive action a user takes — logging a workout, completing a mindfulness exercise, reading an article — moves them forward on a journey, earns tokens, and contributes to something larger than themselves.

Baakhapaa can mirror this exact philosophy for learning:

> **Every lesson watched, every quiz answered, every challenge entered is a positive habit — and the Levels system is how we make that progress visible, rewarding, and addictive.**

The goal is not to make students level up for its own sake. The goal is to make learning feel like a journey worth continuing.

---

## Why Levels Matter

Without a visible progression system, students hit a wall after the initial novelty wears off. Levels solve three specific problems:

| Problem | What Levels Fix |
|---|---|
| "Why should I keep coming back?" | Each login has purpose — progress is always waiting |
| "I feel like everyone is better than me" | Personal progression is always achievable, no matter skill level |
| "I don't know what to do next" | Levels tell students exactly what action to take next |

Betterfly saw a **49% increase in daily active users** and **2× session duration** after implementing their gamified journey. Levels are the backbone of that.

---

## How the Journey Works (The Student Experience)

### Step 1 — Where Am I?

Every student has a **Level** (e.g., Level 1: Seedling, Level 5: Scholar, Level 10: Champion). When they open the app, they can see:

- Their current level name and badge
- A simple progress bar showing how close they are to the next level
- A short, friendly message from the app character (Buddy) telling them exactly what to do next: *"You're 2 challenges away from becoming a Scholar!"*

### Step 2 — How Do I Level Up?

Each level requires completing a set of **simple, achievable actions**. These are not tests — they are habits:

| Example Action | What It Means |
|---|---|
| Watch 3 video lessons | Engage with content |
| Score 80%+ on a quiz | Show understanding |
| Join 1 challenge | Be competitive |
| Log in 5 days in a row | Build a study habit |
| Complete a short video | Explore new topics |

The exact actions for each level are designed to gradually push students toward fuller engagement with the platform — not to be punishingly hard.

### Step 3 — What Do I Get?

Leveling up is a **celebration moment.** When a student reaches a new level:

- A visual animation plays (confetti, level-up badge reveal)
- They receive a **coin reward** (a significant boost, not a small token)
- Their new level badge is visible on their profile and leaderboard
- A new set of actions unlocks for the next level

This mirrors Betterfly's in-app currency visualization — progress feels **tangible and real.**

---

## The Level Tiers

Levels are organized into **named tiers** to give students an identity, not just a number. Inspired by Betterfly's 10-pillar wellbeing system, each tier reflects a learning identity:

| Tier | Levels | Identity | What It Represents |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Seedling** | 1–2 | Just getting started | First lessons watched, first quiz taken |
| **Explorer** | 3–4 | Curious learner | Regular engagement, first challenge entered |
| **Scholar** | 5–6 | Committed student | Consistent habits, strong quiz performance |
| **Champion** | 7–8 | Competitive achiever | Challenge wins, streaks, helping the community |
| **Legend** | 9–10 | Top of the school | Maximum engagement, recognized by teachers and peers |

Each tier unlocks a **new visual badge** and a **unique reward** (e.g., at Scholar tier: a digital certificate; at Legend tier: a school merchandise pack).

---

## The Role of Buddy (The Guide)

Betterfly's most beloved feature is **Buddy** — an avatar that guides, encourages, and celebrates with users through every step of their journey.

Baakhapaa's equivalent is the **Puppet character** already in the app. The Levels system gives Puppet a more active, purposeful role:

- **Before leveling up:** Puppet reminds the student what actions remain: *"Just 1 more quiz to reach Explorer!"*
- **On level up:** Puppet celebrates enthusiastically with an animation
- **If a student is inactive:** Puppet checks in: *"Your progress is waiting for you — come back and keep going!"*
- **At max level:** Puppet congratulates and introduces a special recognition: *"You've reached Legend status. You're one of the best in your school!"*

This makes the app feel like a living companion, not a static tool.

---

## What Teachers and Admins See

Levels are not just motivational for students — they are a **reporting tool** for teachers and admins:

| View | What They See |
|---|---|
| Class overview | Average level of all students in a class |
| Student profile | Individual student's current level and recent activity |
| Level distribution | How many students are at each tier (Seedling → Legend) |
| Progress stagnation | Students who haven't leveled up in 2+ weeks (intervention flag) |

This turns the levels system into an **early warning system** for student disengagement — without requiring teachers to manually track anything.

---

## What's Deliberately Simple at Launch

To ship quickly and learn fast, the first version of Levels keeps it minimal:

**Included at launch:**
- 5–10 defined levels with clear action requirements
- Progress bar visible on the home screen and profile
- Coin reward on level-up
- Buddy/Puppet level-up message
- Basic level badge on student profile

**Saved for later:**
- Animated level-up cutscenes (Phase 2)
- Inter-school level leaderboards (Phase 2)
- Custom level names set by the school (Phase 3)
- Teacher-assigned bonus XP for classroom participation (Phase 3)
- Level-based exclusive content unlocks (Phase 3)

## The Betterfly Parallel: Habits → Impact

Betterfly's core insight was: *people need to see that small actions add up to something meaningful.* They visualized this as **waves of positive change** — your daily workout doesn't just make you healthier, it grows your contribution to causes you care about.

For Baakhapaa, the equivalent is:

> **Each small study action (a quiz, a lesson, a challenge) visibly moves a student closer to a recognized identity — Scholar, Champion, Legend — that their school, teachers, and peers can see.**

The impact is not global, but it is **real and personal**: a student going from Seedling to Scholar is a genuine transformation that a teacher can recognize and celebrate.

Three months after launching the Levels system, we measure:

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| % of students who reach Level 3+ | 60% of active students |
| Average daily sessions per student | +30% vs. pre-levels baseline |
| Student retention at 30 days | 50%+ of students still active |

These mirror Betterfly's post-rebrand results: 20% retention increase, 49% daily active user increase, 2× session duration.

## Summary

The Levels system transforms Baakhapaa from an app students visit occasionally into a **journey they want to continue every day.** By borrowing Betterfly's core philosophy — make habits visible, make progress meaningful, give users a guide — we create the same kind of engagement loop that turned Betterfly's users into daily active participants.

Every quiz is a step. Every lesson is a step. Every challenge is a step. The Levels system makes sure students can **see every single step they take — and feel excited about the next one.**
